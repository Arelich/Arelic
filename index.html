My path in esotericism began at an early age.

In the beginning it was just an interest, I remember how my grandmother took me to church and to various folk healers.

Later, I began to get interested in various rituals and systems of self-knowledge.

Years passed, and my knowledge of different religions, spiritual and magical systems increased. I visited many different temples, holy places, shrines, and places of power.

Along the way, I met many different interesting and unusual people, some of them had really unusual abilities, and some were just an inventor.

Now I want to share my experience and knowledge with those who want to better understand their spiritual nature, and understand their connection with the universe.
